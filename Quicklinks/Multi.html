HTML MultiMedia

  • Common Video Format

    To play videos in web brower we use < video > tag.


    Format File Description
    AVI .avi AVI(Audio Video Interleave). Developed by Microsoft. Commonly used in video cameras and TV hardware. Plays well on Windows computers, but not in web browsers.
    WMV .wmv WMV (Windows Media Video). Developed by Microsoft. Commonly used in video cameras and TV hardware. Plays well on Windows computers, but not in web browsers.
    Flash .flv
    .swf    		 Flash. Developed by Macromedia. Often requires an extra component (plug-in) to play in web browsers.
    MPEG-4 or MP4 .mp4 MP4. Developed by the Moving Pictures Expert Group. Based on QuickTime. Commonly used in newer video cameras and TV hardware. Supported by all HTML5 browsers. Recommended by YouTube.

  • Common Audio Format

    MP3 is the newest format for compressed recorded music. The term MP3 has become synonymous with digital music.
    To play songs in web brower we use < audio > tag.


    Format File Description
    MIDI .mid or .midi AVI(Audio Video Interleave). MIDI (Musical Instrument Digital Interface). Main format for all electronic music devices like synthesizers and PC sound cards. MIDI files do not contain sound, but digital notes that can be played by electronics. Plays well on all computers and music hardware, but not in web browsers.
    WMV .wmv WMV (Windows Media Video). WMA (Windows Media Audio). Developed by Microsoft. Commonly used in music players. Plays well on Windows computers, but not in web browsers.
    MP3 .mp3 MP3 files are actually the sound part of MPEG files. MP3 is the most popular format for music players. Combines good compression (small files) with high quality. Supported by all browsers.
